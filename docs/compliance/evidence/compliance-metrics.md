# Compliance Metrics Evidence

- **Access Review Cadence:** Quarterly access reviews completed with <2% exceptions requiring remediation.
- **Retention SLA:** Automated purge jobs achieve 99.9% on-time completion across regulated regions.

## Explainability KPIs
- **Model Card Freshness:** 100% of production models have model cards updated within 7 days of a release.
- **Attribution Availability:** 99.5% success rate for attribution API calls used by operators and auditors.

## Oversight KPIs
- **Dual Approval Compliance:** 100% of releases include approvals from Responsible AI and product operations stakeholders.
- **Escalation Response Time:** Median response time of 22 minutes from alert to cross-functional triage bridge.

## Resilience KPIs
- **Chaos Exercise Completion:** Quarterly chaos tests executed with corrective actions tracked to closure within 30 days.
- **Failover Readiness:** Active-active regions demonstrate <60s traffic rebalancing during simulated outages.

## Security KPIs
- **Vulnerability SLA:** 100% of critical vulnerabilities patched within 48 hours; high severity within 7 days.
- **Detection Coverage:** 95% of ATT&CK techniques identified as relevant to AI workloads have deployed detections validated through red-team exercises.

Metric owners log source dashboards and export evidence to the compliance data room before each quarterly review.
